I have been studying Web Development & Design since 2018. I have a working understanding of "The Big Three" - HTML, CSS, and Javascript. At present I am taking a 16-credit college certificate program at the County College of Morris (CCM) to sharpen my skills in this challenging and fascinating field.

Web Development

I began learning web development in July 2018. Prior to that I had dabbled in compterish things, earning certificates in Word and Excel. Going back even further, I took a few courses in COBOL, and I remember barely a keystroke today. At the time, I sensed computers just weren't for me - that was in spring of 1993, and the Internet was yet to explode, which it did indeed with the launch of Yahoo in 1994. In the meantime, I pursued other objectives, and now I have come full circle back to tech.

Coursework

Bio

Learning Every Day

I live in northwestern NJ. My first actual solicited project was, naturally enough, for family. The Irish side of the family tree had a stack of family history items scattered about, amd much of it kept getting lost and then reappearing. I was asked to compile and preserve this material, and a website seemed to be the perfect vehicle for this request.

My next experience with design and development came via a request from a friend to help him with a book project. He has since built his own site with Wix, but the experience for me was invaluable. We shared ideas, and I built a prototype site for him. I have kept this site as part of my portfolio and update it regularly with new ideas and formats. Incidentally, his book, called "MAC'S Robot," is a wonderful tale aimed at kids from ages three to five - nevertheless, kids from ages 30 through 50 will enjoy it as well!
